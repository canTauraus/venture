Introduction to 3D Printing

3D printing, also known as additive manufacturing, is the process of creating three-dimensional objects from a digital file.

Applications of 3D Printing

3D printing is used in various industries:

  • Prototyping
  • Healthcare
  • Automotive
  • Consumer Goods

The Future of 3D Printing

The technology is advancing rapidly, enabling more complex and diverse applications of 3D printing.
