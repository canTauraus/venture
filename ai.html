Introduction to AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence.

Applications of AI

AI is used in various fields, including:

  • Machine learning
  • Natural language processing
  • Computer vision
  • Robotics

The Future of AI

AI continues to evolve and has the potential to revolutionize industries and improve our daily lives.
